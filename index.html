  • About our team

    At Bell Bisque, our founders Jake Miller and Harry Mingrone want to share some of the ceramic pieces that are made at Bellarmine’s very own ceramics studio. Both founders have a diverse background in ceramics that has grown profusely after their time working and learning in the studio at Bellarmine. Our website gives a platform for all ceramic students to share their work with the world

    Jake Miller - Co-founder

    Jake Miller, the co-founder of Bell Bisque, has spent two years learning the art of ceramics at Bellarmine. Through the mentorship of his teacher Mr. Huang, Jake has become highly skilled in both wheel work and hand sculpting. His work ranges from bowls, cups, plates, and mugs. Jake’s ever-growing passion for ceramics has led him to create Bell Bisque.

    Harry Mingrone - Co-founder

    Harry Mingrone is a co-founder of Bell Bisque who has taken both Ceramics 1 and Advanced Ceramics: Wheel Throwing at Bellarmine under the instruction of Mr. Huang. Harry possesses a diverse field of skills that include wheel throwing, glazing, and hand building. Harry has developed a deep passion for wheel throwing where he has become a master on the wheel producing plates, bottles, mugs, and cups.

    Why

    We created this website because we feel that ceramics often goes unnoticed on Bellarmine’s campus. We want to give an online platform for all ceramics students to display their hard work that for too long has been ignored.

    Where

    The ceramic studio lies in Sobrato room 101, which is located right by the football field. Please feel free to introduce yourself to teacher Mr. Huang and possibly get a tour of where all of our work is made.

    How

    If you want any of your work displayed on Bell Bisque, email either one of the founders and we will be happy to show your ceramic pieces to the world free of charge.
